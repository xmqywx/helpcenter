# **Trading Fee Structure**

CoinByte offers a competitive and transparent trading fee model. Below is a breakdown of our trading fee structure across different trading modes:

  <table style={{ borderCollapse: 'collapse', width: '100%', fontSize: '0.95rem' }}>
    <thead>

        <th style={{ padding: '12px 10px', whiteSpace: 'nowrap' }}>Type</th>
        <th style={{ padding: '12px 10px', whiteSpace: 'nowrap' }}>Fee</th>
        <th style={{ padding: '12px 10px', textAlign: 'left' }}>Explanation</th>
      </tr>
    </thead>
    <tbody>
      <tr style={{ backgroundColor: '#fff' }}>
        <td style={{ padding: '12px 10px', border: '1px solid #ddd', verticalAlign: 'middle', whiteSpace: 'nowrap' }}>
          Spot Market
        </td>
        <td style={{ padding: '12px 10px', border: '1px solid #ddd', verticalAlign: 'middle', whiteSpace: 'nowrap' }}>
          Taker: 0.1% + spread  
          <br/>
          Maker: 0.1% + spread
        </td>
        <td style={{ padding: '12px 10px', border: '1px solid #ddd', fontSize: '0.92rem' }}>
          <strong>Fee for spot trading = Fee rate × Amount of crypto bought when order was filled.</strong>
          <br/><br/>
          Take USDT/USD as an example. If trader A (0.1% maker fee and 0.15% taker fee, spread is 0.02%) buys 1 USDT at the market price of 100 USD, trading fee = (0.15% + 0.02%) × 1 = 0.0017 USDT. A receives (1 - 0.0017) = 0.9983 USDT.
          <br/><br/>
          If A sells 1 USDT at a limit price and buys 100 USD, A is the maker. The trading fee = (0.1% + 0.02%) × 100 = 0.12 USD. A receives (100 - 0.12) = 99.88 USD.
          <br/><br/>
          <strong>Please note that we provide one of the lowest spreads, but the spread will vary based on the specific Crypto Asset and market liquidity.</strong>
        </td>
      </tr>
      <tr style={{ backgroundColor: '#f6fafa' }}>
        <td style={{ padding: '12px 10px', border: '1px solid #ddd', verticalAlign: 'middle', whiteSpace: 'nowrap' }}>
          Instant Buy, Sell & Swap
        </td>
        <td style={{ padding: '12px 10px', border: '1px solid #ddd', verticalAlign: 'middle', whiteSpace: 'nowrap' }}>
          Buy: 0%  
          <br/>
          Sell: 0%  
          <br/>
          Swap: 0%
        </td>
        <td style={{ padding: '12px 10px', border: '1px solid #ddd', fontSize: '0.95rem' }}>
          <strong>The same calculation applies as Spot Market trade.</strong>
        </td>
      </tr>
    </tbody>
  </table>
</div>

<InfoBox>
<strong>Note:</strong> Spread is the difference between buy and sell price. Please refer to the <a href="/docs/trading/FAQ/spread"><strong>Spread</strong></a> article for more information.
</InfoBox>
