## VIP Program Overview
CoinByte offers a 5-tier VIP program designed to reward loyal and active users with increasing benefits, better trading conditions, and exclusive perks.
  
## VIP Tiers
#### VIP 0 — Builder (Entry Level)
- <strong>Target</strong>: New users
- <strong>Requirement</strong>: None
- <strong>Benefits</strong>:
  - Basic features
  - limits

#### VIP 1 — Builder
- <strong>Assets Requirement</strong>: ≥ A$5,000 (Auto Buy activated)
- <strong>Maintain Requirements</strong>:
  - Maintain an average asset balance of A$3000 or more over the last <strong>30 days</strong>
  - Keep Auto Buy active
- <strong>Benefits</strong>:
  - Faster support

#### VIP 2 — Voyager
- <strong>Assets Requirement</strong>: ≥ A$25,000 (Maintain for 90 days)
- <strong>Maintain Requirements</strong>:
  - Maintain an average asset balance of <strong>A$20,000</strong> or more over the last <strong>30 days</strong>
- <strong>Benefits</strong>:
  - Priority support
  - OTC access
  - Better rates
  - 0.1% Auto Buy cashback

  
#### VIP 3 — Horizon
- <strong>Assets Requirement</strong>: ≥ A$100,000
- <strong>Maintain Requirements</strong>:
  - Maintain an average asset balance of <strong>A$80,000</strong> or more over the last <strong>30 days</strong>
- <strong>Benefits</strong>:
  - Dedicated manager
  - Premium card
  - Private events
  - Beta access
  - 0.2% Auto Buy cashback


#### VIP 4 — Pinnacle (Highest Tier)
- <strong>Assets Requirement</strong>: ≥ A$500k AUM (Invitation only)
- <strong>Maintain Requirements</strong>:
  - Maintain an average asset balance of <strong>A$400,000</strong> or more over the last <strong>90 days</strong>
- <strong>Benefits</strong>:
  - Black card
  - Priority withdrawals
  - OTC pricing
  - Concierge
  - 0.3% Auto Buy cashback
  - VIP Events

<WarningBox>
### Important Notes
- Asset balance is calculated based on your total holdings in AUD.
- Some tiers require maintaining the minimum balance for a specific number of days.
- VIP status is reviewed periodically.
- Higher tiers unlock significantly better fees, support, and exclusive services.
</WarningBox>

## What to do if your Google Authenticator codes aren't working

It’s likely that your device needs to be synced correctly with the time and date. The process to sync the time and date depends on whether you have an Android or an Apple device.

### Android: How to sync the time for Google Authenticator app

<RoundStepList>
<li>Go to the menu in your Google Authenticator app and select **Settings**. </li> 
<li>Select **Time correction for codes**. </li>  
<li>Select **Sync now**.</li> 
</RoundStepList>

### Apple: How to sync the time for all authenticator apps

<RoundStepList>
<li>Go to **Settings** on your iPhone. </li> 
<li>Select **General**, then select **Date & Time**.</li>  
<li>Tap the toggle to the right of **Set Automatically** to turn it on. </li> 
<li>If **Set Automatically** was already turned on, turn it off for a few seconds, then turn it on again.</li>
</RoundStepList>
