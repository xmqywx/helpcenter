# Referral Program

## **What is Referral Program**

To promote our platform, we will provide generous rewards for both the customer who successfully refers a friend and this new customer who joins our platform.

## **How to join in Referral program and get the rewards**

Use your unique referral code to invite friends and family to sign up with CoinByte. When they meet the eligibility requirements, both you and your friend will receive rewards.

To qualify for the reward, each friend must complete email registration, finish KYC verification,  and execute a spot trade or Quick Buy/Sell of no less than 100 AUD within 30 days of registration. Only real executed spot trades and Quick Buy/Sell transactions count toward the valid trading volume.

To learn more about the rewards and detailed rules, please check our <a href="https://support.coinbyte.com.au/docs/announcements/campaigns/referral-program" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" >Refer a Friend Program</a>.

## **What if you or your friend forget to input your referral code when signing up?**

If you forgot to enter the referral code during registration (within 14 days of registering), you can perform the following operations:

<RoundStepList>
<li>If you have a referral code</li>

<li>Go to the <strong>Reward</strong> module in the app.</li>
<li>Select <strong>“Have a referral code to use?”</strong></li>
<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260616learn_rewards_referral2.png" alt="rewards_referral2" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>
<li>On the <strong>Claim Your Rewards</strong> page, you can enter your referral code.</li>
<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260616learn_rewards_referral_code.png" alt="Claim Your Rewards" style={{ width: 300 }} />

</RoundStepList>

## **How it works**

<RoundStepList>

  <li>Visit <a href="https://coinbyte.com.au/en-us/download/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">download the CoinByte App</a>.</li>
  <li>Enter the <strong>Reward</strong> module in the app to copy your unique invitation link/code.</li>
  <li>Share your invitation link with friends.</li>
  <li>Once your friends register, complete KYC, and finish the required trading task within 30 days, both parties will receive the rewards.</li>
