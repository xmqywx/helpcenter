## How to Use Flash?

### What is Flash?

**Flash** is CoinByte’s real-time crypto news center. It provides the latest market updates, project announcements, and analysis to help you stay informed.

### How to Access Flash
  <RoundStepList>
    <li>Tap the <strong>Flash</strong> in the bottom navigation bar.</li>
<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
    <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260609learn_flash_all.jpg" alt="Flash" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>

## Main Flash Page
<RoundStepList>
<li>Switch between tabs: All、Cryptos、Market Moves、Coin Events、Listings、Risk Alerts、Market Themes、On - chain、Macro. </li>
<li>Toggle <strong>Important Only</strong> to see only high-priority news.</li>
<li>Live timestamp shows real-time updates.</li>

## Broadcast Settings
You can customize how Flash delivers news by going to <strong>Broadcast Settings</strong>.

<RoundStepList>
<li>If you select <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260609learn_iocn_speaker.png" alt="speaker icon" width="20" /> here, you will be in Broadcast Settings.</li>
<li>Adjust the following options:</li>

</RoundStepList>

<ul>

<li><strong>Flash Broadcast</strong>: Main switch to enable/disable all Flash updates.</li>
<li><strong>Important Only</strong>: Enable this to receive only critical news and alerts.</li>
<li><strong>Background Playback</strong>: Allow Flash to continue updating even when the app is running in the background.</li>
<li><strong>Speed</strong>: Use the slider to choose how fast news content scrolls or updates (Slow / Normal / Fast).</li>

</ul>

## How to Manage Flash Channels

### What are Channels?

Channels allow you to personalize the news you see in <strong>Flash</strong>. You can choose which topics to follow, reorder them, or hide ones you’re not interested in.

### How to Access Channels

<RoundStepList>

  <li>Open the <strong>Flash</strong> page and tap the <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260609learn_iocn_list" alt="speaker icon" width="20" /> icon in the top right corner.</li>
  <li>Tap the <strong>Channels</strong>  icon or menu (usually located near the top right).</li>
  <figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
    <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260609learn_flash_channels.png" alt="Channels" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>

</RoundStepList>

### Managing Your Channels
<RoundStepList>
    <li>Reorder: Long-press and drag channels up or down to change their display order.</li>
    <li>Hide a Channel: Tap the <strong>×</strong> icon on the right of any channel in "My Channels".</li>
    <li>Add New Channels: Scroll to <strong>More Channels</strong> and tap the <strong>+</strong> button next to the channel you want.</li>
</RoundStepList>
    Customizing your channels helps you focus on the market topics most relevant to you, such as Bitcoin news, new listings, or on-chain developments.
