# Explore your Holding Asset

CoinByte provides a comprehensive way to help you quickly and intuitively understand your current asset situation. Below, we will provide a detailed introduction to our platform's asset page and how to use it.

## Explore your total asset

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="Mobile App" label="App">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **App**

<div style={{ marginBottom: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
<li>Select <strong>Asset</strong> in footer.</li>
<li>You can explore the status of all your held assets, including the total est value in your quote Currency，est value of each asset in your quote currency.</li>
<li>Select <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260608learn_iocn_record_.png" alt="Transaction history icon" width="30" /><strong>Transaction history</strong> , you can view the holding quantity, In use, available and convert to fiat.</li>
