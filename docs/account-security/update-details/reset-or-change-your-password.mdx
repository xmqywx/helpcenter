Regularly updating your password enhances security and prevents unauthorized access.  
Weak or outdated passwords can make your account more susceptible to compromise.

Learn more about <a href="/docs/account-security/create-coinbyte-account/password-requirements-and-management"><strong>Password Requirements and Management</strong></a> to create the strongest possible password.

---

## Reset a Forgotten Password
<Tabs groupId="platform">
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile App">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **App**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>
<InfoBox>If you can't access to your account, you can reset your password.</InfoBox>

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select <strong>Forgotten password?</strong> from the password entry page.</li>
<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260702support_enter_password.png" alt="enter password" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>
  <li>Type your registered <strong>Email address</strong> and click the <strong>Continue</strong> button.</li>

  <li>Complete the three security verifications: <strong>Phone</strong>, <strong>Email</strong>, and <strong>Authenticator App</strong>.</li>
<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260702support_reset_password_security.png" alt="reset password security" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>

  <li>Enter your <strong>new password</strong>, <strong>confirm your password</strong>, then click the <strong>Continue</strong> button.</li>
  <li>Finally, return to the login page, enter the <strong>new password</strong> you just changed, and log in.</li>

</RoundStepList>

#### If you are unable to complete the three verifications, please follow the steps below:

<RoundStepList>
  <li>please select <strong>Security verification unavailable?</strong></li>
<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260703support_reset_password_security.png" alt="reset_password" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>
  <li>Select the verification option you are unable to complete.</li>
<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260703support_reset_password_not_verfication.png" alt="not verfication" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>
  <li>For example: <strong>Can't receive SMS code</strong>.</li>
  <li>Fill in the required information on the current page, then select the <strong>Submit</strong> button.</li>
<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260703support_password_2fa_removal.png" alt="2fa removal" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>

</RoundStepList>

<WarningBox>

💡Review Process：

<ul>
  <li>Review takes 1-3 business days. The result will be sent to the contact email you provided.</li>
  <li>We reply on business days between 10:00-19:00 in the order received.</li>
  <li>Please ensure your contact email is correct — the reply will be sent there.</li>
</ul>

</WarningBox>

  </TabItem>

  <TabItem value="web" label="Web">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **Web**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<InfoBox>If you can't access to your account, you can reset your password.</InfoBox>
<CoinByteButton text="Reset your Password" link="https://coinbyte.com.au/en-us/reset-pwd/" />

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select <strong>Forgot password?</strong> from the sign-in page.</li>
 <figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
      <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/account-security/update-details/reset-or-change-your-password/2.png')} alt="Step 1 reset password" width="500" />
    </figure>
  <li>Type your registered email address.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Get code</strong>, you will receive an email to get the <strong>verification code</strong>.</li>
  <li>Finish your <strong>email verification</strong> and <strong>Google verification</strong> into the CoinByte website. Click <strong>Confirmation</strong>.</li>
 <figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/account-security/update-details/reset-or-change-your-password/3.png')} alt="Step 4 reset password" width="500" />
    </figure>
  <li>Enter and save a new password before returning to the sign-in page.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Change Your Password

<Tabs groupId="platform">
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile App">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **App**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>
<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select the <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260604learn_iocn_personal.png" alt="CoinByte icon" width="20" /> in the top right to open the <strong>Personal center</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select your <strong>email address</strong> to open the <strong>Account information page</strong>.</li>
<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260604learn_iocn_personal1.jpg" alt="personal" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>
  <li>Select <strong>Security</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Password</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter your current password, Enter your new password, and finally confirm password.</li>

<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260609learn_change_password.png" alt="change password" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>

  <li>Select the <strong>Confirm</strong> button belowto finish.</li>

</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>

  <TabItem value="web" label="Web">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **Web**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>
<InfoBox>If you can access to your account, you can change your current password.</InfoBox>
<CoinByteButton text="Change your password" link="https://coinbyte.com.au/en-us/user/set-pwd/" />

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select the <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260604learn_iocn_personal.png" alt="CoinByte icon" width="20" />  in the header to open the <strong>Personal center</strong> page.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Change</strong> on the right of <strong>Password</strong>.</li>
  <li>Type your current password and new password.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Change</strong> to finish.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

---

<WarningBox>
<strong>Please Note:</strong> For security purposes, you cannot withdraw funds or crypto within 24 hours after resetting or change your password.
</WarningBox>
