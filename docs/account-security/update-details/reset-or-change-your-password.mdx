Regularly updating your password enhances security and prevents unauthorized access.  
Weak or outdated passwords can make your account more susceptible to compromise.

Learn more about <a href="/docs/account-security/create-coinbyte-account/password-requirements-and-management"><strong>Password Requirements and Management</strong></a> to create the strongest possible password.

---

## Reset a Forgotten Password
<Tabs groupId="platform">
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile App">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **App**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>
<InfoBox>If you can't access to your account, you can reset your password.</InfoBox>

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select <strong>Forgotten your password?</strong> from the sign-in page.</li>

  <li>Type your registered email address.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Next Step</strong>, you will receive an email to <strong>get the verification code</strong>.</li>
  <li>Finish the <strong>email verification</strong> and <strong>Google verification</strong>. Click <strong>Next Step</strong>.</li>

<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/account-security/update-details/reset-or-change-your-password/1.png')}
    alt="Step 1 Reset Password"
    style={{ width: 300 }}
  />
</figure>

  <li>Enter and save a new password before returning to the sign-in page.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>

  <TabItem value="web" label="Web">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **Web**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<InfoBox>If you can't access to your account, you can reset your password.</InfoBox>
<CoinByteButton text="Reset your Password" link="https://coinbyte.com.au/en-us/reset-pwd/" />

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select <strong>Forgot password?</strong> from the sign-in page.</li>
 <figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
      <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/account-security/update-details/reset-or-change-your-password/2.png')} alt="Step 1 reset password" width="500" />
    </figure>
  <li>Type your registered email address.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Get code</strong>, you will receive an email to get the <strong>verification code</strong>.</li>
  <li>Finish your <strong>email verification</strong> and <strong>Google verification</strong> into the CoinByte website. Click <strong>Confirmation</strong>.</li>
 <figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/account-security/update-details/reset-or-change-your-password/3.png')} alt="Step 4 reset password" width="500" />
    </figure>
  <li>Enter and save a new password before returning to the sign-in page.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Change Your Password (Web only)

<Tabs groupId="platform">
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile App">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **App**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>
<InfoBox>If you can access to your account, you can change your current password.</InfoBox>
Currently, password changes are only supported on the web platform. 

  </TabItem>

  <TabItem value="web" label="Web">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **Web**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>
<InfoBox>If you can access to your account, you can change your current password.</InfoBox>
<CoinByteButton text="Change your password" link="https://coinbyte.com.au/en-us/user/set-pwd/" />

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select the <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/general/profile-center-web.png')} alt="profile icon" width="25" /> in the header to open the <strong>Personal center</strong> page.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Change</strong> on the right of <strong>Password</strong>.</li>
  <li>Type your current password and new password.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Change</strong> to finish.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

---

<WarningBox>
<strong>Please Note:</strong> For security purposes, you cannot withdraw funds or crypto within 24 hours after resetting or change your password.
</WarningBox>
