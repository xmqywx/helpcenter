It's crucial to keep your registered phone number current for SMS confirmation and verification.

If your number changes and you still have access to your old registered number, you can follow the step-by-step instructions in this article.

<InfoBox>
ðŸ’¡ If you no longer have access to your old number, you can change it through another 2FA.  
</InfoBox>

## How to change your phone number

<Tabs groupId="platform">
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile App">

<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **App**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select the <strong>CoinByte icon</strong> <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/genral/logo_app.png')} alt="CoinByte icon" width="20" /> in the top left to open the <strong>Personal center</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Security Settings</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Cellphone verification</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>change</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter the one-time passcode sent to the current phone number on your account and select <strong>Next Step</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter the new phone number and select <strong>Next Step</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter the one-time passcode sent to the new phone number and select <strong>Verify</strong>.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="web" label="Web">

<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **Web**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/genralprofile-center-web')} alt="Web profile icon" width="25" /> in the right of header to open the <strong>Personal Center </strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Security Settings</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Settings</strong> in <strong>SMS certification</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Change</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter the one-time passcode sent to the current phone number on your account and select <strong>Next Step</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter the new phone number and select <strong>Next Step</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter the one-time passcode sent to the new phone number and select <strong>Verify</strong>.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

<WarningBox>
ðŸ’¡ <strong>Note:</strong> After changing your phone number, you will not be able to withdraw currencies within 24 hours.
</WarningBox>
