It's crucial to keep your registered phone number current for SMS confirmation and verification.

If your number changes and you still have access to your old registered number, you can follow the step-by-step instructions in this article.

<InfoBox>
💡 If you no longer have access to your old number, you can change it through another 2FA.  
</InfoBox>

---

## How to change your phone number

<Tabs groupId="platform">
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile App">

<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **App**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select the <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260604learn_iocn_personal.png" alt="CoinByte icon" width="20" /> in the top right to open the <strong>Personal center</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select your <strong>email address</strong> to open the <strong>Account information page</strong>.</li>
<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260604learn_iocn_personal1.jpg" alt="personal" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>
  <li>Select <strong>Security</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Phone Number</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select the number on the right <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260604learn_iocn_edit.png" alt="edit" width="20" />.</li>
  <li>Enter the number you want to change, and click <strong>Send verification code</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter the one-time passcode sent to the current phone number on your account and select <strong>Next Step</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter the new phone number and select <strong>Next Step</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter the one-time passcode sent to the new phone number and select <strong>Verify</strong>.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="web" label="Web">

<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **Web**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<CoinByteButton text="Change your Phone Number" link="https://coinbyte.com.au/en-us/user/bind-phone/" />

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260604learn_iocn_personal.png" alt="Web profile icon" width="30" /> <strong>Profile</strong>in the right of header to open the <strong>Personal Center </strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Security Settings</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Settings</strong> > Phone Verification > <strong>Edit</strong>.</li>
<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260604learn_iocn_phone.png" alt="Edit" style={{ width: 600 }} />
</figure>
  <li>Enter the number you want to change, and click <strong>Get code</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter the one-time passcode sent to the new phone number and select <strong>Verify</strong>.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

<WarningBox>
💡 <strong>Note:</strong> After changing your phone number, you will not be able to withdraw currencies within 24 hours.
</WarningBox>

### What if you don't have access to your registered Phone Number?

If you’ve lost access to your device or can’t log in with Phone Number please refer to the  <a href="https://support.coinbyte.com.au/docs/account-security/update-details/update-account-info"><strong>Update Account Inf</strong></a> section to modify your Phone Number.
