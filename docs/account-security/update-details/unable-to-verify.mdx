#### Unable to Complete Verification?

Forgetting or losing access to your registered email, phone number, or Authenticator App is a common issue that can lock you out of your account.


### When You Need This Recovery Process

<ul>
  <li>You no longer have access to the old email.</li>
  <li>You have changed or lost your phone number and can’t receive SMS codes.</li>
  <li>Your Authenticator App is lost, the phone has been reset, or the app is unavailable.</li>
  <li>Normal login or verification methods all fail.</li>
</ul>

In these cases, you cannot use the standard “change” options and must go through the <strong>Security Verification Unavailable</strong> route.

---

#### Best Practices to Avoid Future Issues

<ul>
  <li>Use an Authenticator App with cloud backup (such as Authy) instead.</li>
  <li>Use an Authenticator App with cloud backup (such as Authy) instead of relying solely on SMS or email.</li>
  <li>Regularly review and update your bound contact information.</li>
  <li>Store account recovery codes and backup keys safely offline.</li>
</ul>
