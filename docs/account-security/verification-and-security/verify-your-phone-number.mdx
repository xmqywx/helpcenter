Your phone number is also one of the methods of **2FA (Two-Factor Authentication)**.
<InfoBox>
After you verify your Phone Number, you will need to <a href="/docs/account-security/verification-and-security/complete-your-KYC-verification-(individual-account)"><strong>Complete your KYC Verification (individual account)</strong></a> or <a href="/docs/account-security/verification-and-security/complete-your-KYC-verification-(corporate-account)"><strong>Complete your KYC Verification (corporate account)</strong></a>.
</InfoBox>

---

## How to verify your phone number

<Tabs groupId="platform">
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile App">
  <div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **App**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select the <strong>green CoinByte icon</strong> <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/general/logo_app.png')} alt="CoinByte App Icon" width="20" /> in the top left to open the Personal center.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Security Settings</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Cellphone verification</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter your phone number and click <strong>Get a code</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter the one-time passcode sent to the phone number and select <strong>Next Step</strong>.</li>
</RoundStepList>

 
  </TabItem>

  <TabItem value="web" label="Web">
  <div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **Web**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
    <li>Select the <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/general/profile-center-web.png')} alt="profile icon" width="30" /> in the header to open the Personal center page.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Security</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Settings</strong> in <strong>Phone verification</strong>.</li>
   <figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/account-security/verification-and-security/verify-your-phone-number/1.png')} alt="1" style={{ width: 500 }} />
</figure>
  <li>Enter your phone number and click <strong>Get code</strong>.</li>
 <figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/account-security/verification-and-security/verify-your-phone-number/2.png')} alt="2" style={{ width: 500 }} />
</figure>
  <li>Enter the one-time passcode sent to the phone number and select <strong>Continue</strong>.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

<InfoBox>
After you have verified 2FA, you will need to <a href="/docs/account-security/verification-and-security/complete-your-KYC-verification-(individual-account)"><strong>Complete your KYC Verification (individual account)</strong></a> or <a href="/docs/account-security/verification-and-security/complete-your-KYC-verification-(corporate-account)"><strong>Complete your KYC Verification (corporate account)</strong></a>.
</InfoBox>
