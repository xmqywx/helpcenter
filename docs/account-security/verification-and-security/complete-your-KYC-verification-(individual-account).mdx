We conduct identity verification checks to confirm that individuals are who they claim to be. This is a crucial step in setting up your CoinByte account.

As required by law, we must verify the identity of all customers wishing to deposit or withdraw Australian or New Zealand dollars on our platform. Details can refer to here.

<WarningBox>
This section is specially for individual account. If you are a corporate user, please refer to <a href="/docs/account-security/verification-and-security/complete-your-KYC-verification-corporate-account">Complete your KYC Verification (corporate account)</a>.
</WarningBox>

---

## The required documents of your initial ID verification

<TickList>
  <li>One of your government-issued ID.</li>
  <li>One proof of address document.</li>
  <li>One selfie photo of yourself.</li>
</TickList>

---

## Accepted identity documents

<TickList>
  <li>Passport</li>
  <li>Driving license</li>
  <li>ID card</li>
  <li>Residence permit</li>
</TickList>

---

## Accepted proof of address documents

<TickList>
  <li>Payslips</li>
  <li>Council rates notice</li>
  <li>Water, gas, or electricity bill</li>
  <li>Tenancy or rental agreement</li>
  <li>Government-issued photo identification card</li>
  <li>Any Government correspondence</li>
  <li>Inland Revenue Notice (New Zealand)</li>
  <li>A paper bank statement you received in the mail from your bank (We can't accept printed copies of online bank statements)</li>
</TickList>

## How to complete your initial ID verification

<Tabs groupId="platform">
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile App">

<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **App**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select the <strong>CoinByte icon</strong> <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/general/logo_app.png')} alt="logo" width="20" /> in the top left to open the Personal center.</li>
  <li>Select your <strong>email address</strong> to open the <strong>Account information page</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Identity Verification Status</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Individual account</strong>.</li>
  <li>Follow the prompts and take photos of the required documents to complete the verification.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="web" label="Web">

<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **Web**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<CoinByteButton text="Complete your Identity Verification" link="https://coinbyte.com.au/en-us/user/verify/" />

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select the <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/general/profile-center-web.png')} alt="profile icon" width="30" /> <strong>Profile</strong> in the right of header to open the Personal center.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Verification</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Individual account</strong>.</li>
  <li>Follow the prompts and take photos of the required documents to complete the verification.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
</Tabs>
