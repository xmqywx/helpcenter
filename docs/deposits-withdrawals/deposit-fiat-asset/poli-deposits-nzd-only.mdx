POLi is a payment system used for New Zealand dollars. POLi allows you to make direct payments to CoinByte without needing a card.

<WarningBox>
ðŸ’¡ <strong>Please note:</strong><br/>
<TickList>
  <li>Deposits to your CoinByte account must come from a POLi account registered under the same name as your CoinByte account.</li>
  <li>POLi deposits are available exclusively to New Zealand dollars.</li>
</TickList>
</WarningBox>

## Which banks are supported?

The following New Zealand banks are able to make POLi payments to CoinByte:

<TickList>
  <li>ANZ</li>
  <li>ASB</li>
  <li>Bank of New Zealand</li>
  <li>Kiwibank</li>
  <li>TSB Bank</li>
  <li>Westpac</li>
</TickList>

The list of supported banks is managed by the POLi payment system â€” <a href="https://www.polipayments.com/SupportedBanks" target="_blank"><strong>learn more about which banks can make POLi payments</strong></a>.

If your bank should be supported but doesn't appear in the dropdown list, it may be undergoing maintenance for its POLi integration. You can <a href="https://status.polipayments.com/" target="_blank"><strong>Check the live status of POLi</strong></a>.

## How to deposit funds with POLi

<Tabs groupId="platform">
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile App">

<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### App

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select <strong>Deposit</strong> at the homepage.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Fiat deposit</strong>, then select <strong>NZD</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>POLi</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter the amount you want to deposit, select <strong>Continue</strong>.</li>
  <li>Then the screen will jump to Finmo checkout page, select <strong>Proceed</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select your Bank Account, continue to proceed.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="web" label="Web">

<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### Web

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select <strong>Asset</strong> on the header.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Deposit</strong> on the left hand side menu.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>NZD</strong> on the top of the page.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>POLi</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter the amount you want to deposit, select <strong>Continue</strong>.</li>
  <li>Then the screen will jump to Finmo checkout page, select <strong>Proceed</strong>.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## How long do POLi transfers take?

- Usually instant

---

## Limits

- **Minimum deposit amount:** $10 NZD
- **Daily Deposit Limit:** $50,000 NZD

---

## Fees for POLi deposits

- Free
