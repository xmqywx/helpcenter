## **How to Deposit Funds via Bank Transfer**

### **1. Find Your CoinByte Bank Transfer Details**

#### App

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select <strong>Deposit</strong> in the <strong>Dashboard</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Fiat Deposit</strong>, then choose <strong>AUD</strong> or <strong>NZD</strong>.</li>
  <li>Copy the deposit information provided.</li>
</RoundStepList>

#### Web

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select <strong>Asset</strong> in the header.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Deposit</strong> from the left-hand menu.</li>
  <li>Choose the currency you wish to deposit at the top of the page.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Bank Transfer</strong> and copy the deposit information provided.</li>
</RoundStepList>

### **2. Initiate a New Deposit from Your Bank**

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Log in to your online banking and create a new bank transfer.</li>
  <li>Enter the CoinByte bank details obtained from the deposit screen.</li>
  <li>You may enter any information in the reference field for your records. The deposit will still be processed successfully without a reference.</li>
  <li>Enter the amount you wish to transfer and complete the transaction.</li>
</RoundStepList>

## **Processing Times for Bank Transfers**

<TickList>
  <li><strong>Osko-enabled banks</strong>: Typically within a minute.</li>
  <li><strong>Non-Osko banks</strong>: May take 2-3 business days.</li>
</TickList>

### **Deposit Delays**

<TickList>
  <li><strong>First-time deposits</strong>: May take up to 24 business hours due to bank security protocols.</li>
  <li><strong>CBA/Bankwest Policy</strong>: Commonwealth Bank (CBA) and Bankwest impose a 24-hour holding period for all crypto exchange deposits, regardless of your deposit history. This is a standard banking security measure.</li>
</TickList>

### **If Your Deposit Is Delayed**

<TickList>
  <li>Check the status of the deposit in your online banking. If it shows as 'Pending', it is still with your bank.</li>
  <li>Contact your bank to inquire about the delay.</li>
  <li>Note that banking business hours apply. For example, deposits made on a Friday or over the weekend may arrive by the following Monday.</li>
  <li>If you still cannot determine why your deposit hasn't reached your CoinByte account, please contact us via live chat.</li>
</TickList>

## **Deposit Limits**

**Minimum deposit amount:** 
$10 AUD or $10 NZD.

**Daily Deposit Limit:** 
$50k AUD or $50k NZD each day.

---

## **Fees for Bank Transfer Deposits**

<InfoBox>
CoinByte does not charge any fees for deposits. However, your financial institution may impose a fee.
</InfoBox>

- <strong>Free</strong>

We may request additional verification if we detect suspicious deposits in your account.
