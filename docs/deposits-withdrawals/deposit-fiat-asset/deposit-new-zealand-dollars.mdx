You can fund your CoinByte account with New Zealand dollars through bank transfer and **POLi**.

---

## How long do transfers take?

<TickList>
<li><strong>Your first NZD deposits</strong> may take up to 24 business hours to process due to security protocols implemented by banks.</li>
<li><strong>Banking business hours are observed.</strong> Deposits initiated on Fridays, weekends or holidays will typically be processed on the next business day (e.g., Monday).</li>
<li><strong>CBA/Bankwest Policy:</strong> Commonwealth Bank (CBA) and its affiliated brand, Bankwest enforce a 24-hour holding period for all crypto exchange deposits, irrespective of your deposit history. This is a standard banking security measure.</li>
</TickList>

---

## Standard Transfer Times:

<TickList>
<li><strong>Direct deposits/Bank transfer</strong>: Depends on banks, it may take up to 2–3 business days.</li>
<li><strong>POLi</strong>: Usually instant.</li>
</TickList>

## Deposit limits

<InfoBox>
Limits include both cash and crypto transactions.
</InfoBox>

**Minimum Deposit:** 
$10 NZD each deposit.

**Daily Deposit Limit:** 
$50k NZD each day.

---

## Deposit fees

<InfoBox>
CoinByte didn’t charge any fee for deposits, but your financial institution may charge a fee.
</InfoBox>

**Direct deposits/Bank transfer**  
- Free

**POLi**  
- Free
