# Refer a Friend Program

Dear CoinByte users,

The new <strong>Refer a Friend Program</strong> is now live, offering users a simple and rewarding way to earn attractive bonuses by inviting friends to join the CoinByte community.

### 1. Rules

<strong>Eligibility Conditions</strong>: The invitee must complete email registration, finish KYC verification, and execute a spot trade or Quick Buy/Sell of no less than 100 AUD within 30 days after registration.Only real executed spot trades and Quick Buy/Sell transactions count toward the valid trading volume.

#### Campaign details:

<div align="center">

| Tier | Number of Qualified Invitees | Inviter Reward | Invitee Reward |
|:----:|:----------------------------:|:--------------:|:--------------:|
| Starter | 0 | A$50 | A$50 |
| Vip 1 | 5 | A$55 | A$50 |
| Vip 2 | 10 | A$60 | A$50 |
| Vip 3 | 20 | A$70 | A$50 |
| Vip 4 | 35 | A$85 | A$50 |
| Vip 5 | 60 | A$100 | A$50 |

</div>

---

<strong>Supplementary Rules</strong>: The supplementary period is 14 days. If the invitee forgets to enter the invitation code, they can still input the code within 14 days of registration, and it will remain valid. Even if the required tasks were completed before entering the invitation code, the invitation will still be considered successful and rewards will be issued.

<InfoBox>
<strong>Important Condition</strong>: The invitee’s registration time must be later than the inviter’s registration time for the invitation relationship to be valid.
</InfoBox>


---

### 2. Refer a Friend Program

The Refer a Friend Program is designed for CoinByte users to easily grow their network while earning generous rewards. By inviting friends to join CoinByte, both the inviter and the invitee can enjoy rich bonuses and exciting on-chain benefits.

---

### 3. How to Participate

<RoundStepList>

  <li>Visit <a href="https://coinbyte.com.au/en-us/download/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">download the CoinByte App</a>.</li>
  <li>Enter the <strong>Reward</strong> module in the app to copy your unique invitation link/code.</li>
  <li>Share your invitation link with friends.</li>
  <li>Once your friends register, complete KYC, and finish the required trading task within 30 days, both parties will receive the rewards.</li>

</RoundStepList>
