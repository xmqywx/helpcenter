# Referral Program FAQ

Log in to your CoinByte account to check whether the products, features, rules, and terms in this article apply to you.

### Personalized Referral Rewards

Invite your friends to join CoinByte and receive exclusive rewards.
<ul>

  <li>During each referral campaign, the rewards you receive may vary. You may earn fixed crypto rewards or various rewards with a total value of up to a certain amount per successful referral.</li>
  <li>Log in to your CoinByte App and visit the Reward module to check the latest reward offers you are eligible for.</li>

</ul>

### Eligibility for Referral Rewards

Invite your friends to create an CoinByte account using your referral code. After they complete the following tasks, you will be eligible for rewards:

<ul>
  <li>Sign up through your unique referral link or code.</li>
  <li>Complete advanced identity verification (KYC).</li>
  <li>Complete a deposit and trading task: Your friend must complete a spot trade or Quick Buy/Sell worth no less than <strong>100 AUD</strong> within 30 days of registration.</li>
</ul>

### FAQ

#### How do I invite friends?

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Log in to your CoinByte App and go to the <strong>Reward</strong> module.</li>
  <li>Copy your referral code or invitation link and share it with your friends.</li>
</RoundStepList>


#### Note:

<ul>
  <li>You can check your referral rewards in the CoinByte App → <strong>Reward</strong> → <strong>Completed Referrals</strong>, then selecting <strong>Referral Details</strong>.</li>
  <li>Your friend’s reward must be claimed within the current campaign period.</li>
  <li>You can invite as many friends as you like, the more friends you invite, the more rewards you get.</li>
  <li>Your friends will automatically join the platform’s New User Bonus Program.</li>
  <li>Referral relationships can only be established at the time of registration. Once registration and identity verification are complete, the relationship cannot be changed.</li>
</ul>

### Can my invitees add or change a referral code after registering?
Yes. If you did not enter a referral code during registration, you can still add one within 14 days of creating your account. As long as the referral is valid and you meet the promotion requirements, you will remain eligible to receive the applicable rewards.

#### How do inviters earn rewards?
<ul>
  <li>Inviters receive rewards once their invitees complete all required tasks.</li>
  <li>Rewards will be automatically credited to your account after you meet the campaign requirements.</li>
  <li>Inviters may unlock higher rewards if their invitees complete additional or more advanced tasks.</li>
</ul>

#### Are referral rewards the same for all inviters?
Rewards may vary depending on the campaign and the tasks required. The exact reward structure is always displayed on the <strong>Reward</strong> page in your account.
