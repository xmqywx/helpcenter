# Scheduled Platform Maintenance

We regularly perform maintenance to ensure the best possible experience for our users.

## Maintenance Schedule

### Regular Maintenance Windows
- **Weekly**: Every Tuesday, 2:00 AM - 4:00 AM UTC
- **Monthly**: First Sunday of each month, 12:00 AM - 6:00 AM UTC

## What to Expect During Maintenance

During scheduled maintenance periods:
- Trading may be temporarily unavailable
- Deposits and withdrawals may be delayed
- Account access might be limited

## How We Keep You Informed

1. **Email Notifications**: Sent 48 hours before major maintenance
2. **In-App Alerts**: Displayed 24 hours prior to maintenance
3. **Status Page**: Real-time updates at status.coinbyte.com

## Emergency Maintenance

In rare cases, emergency maintenance may be required:
- We'll notify users as soon as possible
- Updates will be posted on all our communication channels
- Our team works to minimize downtime

## Questions?

If you have any questions about maintenance schedules, please contact our support team.
