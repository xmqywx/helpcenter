## How to view your Account Statement

You can download your <strong>last 12 monthly account statements</strong> from the <strong>Account Statement</strong> page.

<Tabs groupId="platform">
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile App">
  <div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

### **App**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>
<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select the <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260604learn_iocn_personal.png"  alt="Personal Center" width="20" /> in the top left to open the Personal center page.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Statement</strong>.</li>
<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260608learn_account_statement1.jpg" alt="Statement" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>
  <li>This displays the last 12 monthly account statements.</li>
  <li>Each month's bill is settled on the last day of the month.</li>
  <li>Click to download one of the month's statements.</li>
<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260608learn_account_statement2.png" alt="Account Statement" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>

  <li>The <strong>Statement</strong> section provides an overview of your cryptocurrency holdings and their current value.</li>
  <li>The page displays the following information for each asset:</li>

- **Crypto** – Crypto in the account
- **Equity Balance** – The quantity of the asset you currently own.
- **Closing Price in AUD** – The latest closing price of the asset in Australian dollars.
- **Equity Value in AUD** – The total value of your holdings in Australian dollars, calculated based on your balance and the closing price.

<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src="https://efile.coinbyte.com.au/20260608learn_account_statement3.jpg" alt="Account Statement" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>
</RoundStepList>

You can use the Asset Summary to quickly review your portfolio and monitor the value of your assets.
