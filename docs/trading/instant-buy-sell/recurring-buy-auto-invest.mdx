# Recurring Buy (Auto-invest)

Schedule repeating orders that process automatically and start building your portfolio today.

<InfoBox>

<TickList>

## How to create Recurring Buy (Auto-invest)

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="Mobile App" label="App">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **App**

<div style={{ marginBottom: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
<li>Select <strong>Auto-invest</strong> in the <strong>Dashboard</strong> to navigate to the <strong>Recurring Buy</strong> section.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Create a plan</strong>.</li>
<li>Select the crypto you want to buy.</li>
<li>Set the amount of fiat currency to invest per order.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Invest Frequency</strong>. The first purchase time will be <strong>30 minutes</strong> after setting up the recurring order.</li>
<li>Before you continue, you need to make sure you've read and agree the <strong><a href="/docs/user-guide/legal/recurring-buy-agreement">Recurring Buy Agreement</a></strong>. </li>
<li>Click <strong>Continue</strong>, review your order, and finish.</li>
</RoundStepList>

</TabItem>

<TabItem value="Web Platform" label="Web">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **Web**

<div style={{ marginBottom: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
<li>Select <strong>Recurring buy</strong> in the <strong>header</strong> to navigate to the <strong>Recurring Buy</strong> section.</li>

<li>Select <strong>Create Plan</strong>.</li>
<CoinByteButton text="Create Plan Now" link="https://coinbyte.com.au/en-us/invest/submit" />
<li>Select the crypto you want to buy.</li>
<li>Set the amount of fiat currency to invest per order.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Invest Frequency</strong>. The <strong>first purchase time will be 30 minutes</strong> after setting up the recurring order.</li>
<li>Before you continue, you need to make sure you've read and agree the <strong><a href="/docs/user-guide/legal/recurring-buy-agreement">Recurring Buy Agreement</a></strong>. </li>
<li>Click <strong>Continue</strong>, review your order, and finish.</li>
</RoundStepList>

## How to resume or pause a Recurring Buy (Auto-invest)

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="Mobile App" label="App">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **App**

<div style={{ marginBottom: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
<li>Select <strong>Active plan</strong> in the <strong>Recurring Buy</strong> page.</li>
<li>Select the plan you want to operate.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Stop plan</strong> or <strong>Resume plan</strong>.</li>
</RoundStepList>

</TabItem>
