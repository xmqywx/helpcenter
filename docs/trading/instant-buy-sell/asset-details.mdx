## Bitcoin (BTC) Asset Details Page

The <strong>BTC Asset Details Page</strong> is your dedicated screen to view real-time Bitcoin price, performance charts, account balance, and detailed project information.

## 1.Top Section

- **Coin Name & Icon**: Displays the official Bitcoin (BTC) icon and name.
- **Current Price**: Shows the live price of Bitcoin in your local fiat currency .
- **Price Change**: Displays the price movement over the past day (24h), including the amount and percentage change (shown in green for gains).
- **Price Chart**: Interactive glowing price chart. Switch between timeframes using the tabs: Live, 1D, 1W, 1M, 3M, 1Y. High (H) and Low (L) values are clearly labeled.

## 2.Your Holdings

Shows your current Bitcoin balance in both fiat value and BTC amount.

## 3.Quick Action Buttons

- **Auto Buy**: Set up scheduled recurring purchases of Bitcoin (dollar-cost averaging).
- **Buy**: Quick button to buy Bitcoin.
- **Receive**: Display your personal BTC deposit address to receive transfers.

## 4.Stats

Provides an overview of the project:
