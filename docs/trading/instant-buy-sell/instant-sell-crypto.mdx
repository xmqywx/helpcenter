# Instant sell crypto

Selling your crypto on CoinByte is a straightforward process. CoinByte supports most kinds of crypto selling.  
You can sell crypto instantly for <strong>Australian dollars</strong> and <strong>New Zealand dollars</strong>. Make sure that you have selected the correct base currency.

## How to instantly sell crypto on CoinByte

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="Mobile App" label="App">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **App**

<div style={{ marginBottom: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
<li>Select <strong>Buy/Sell</strong> on the bottom tool bar.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Sell</strong>.</li>
<li>Select the <strong>Trading pairs (Quote Currency/Base Currency)</strong> that you want to trade on the top of the page. For example, ETH/AUD.</li>
<li>Enter the amount of <strong>crypto</strong> you want to sell, or</li>

<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
   <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/tradinig/instant-buy-sell/instant-sell-crypto/1.png')} alt="Sell crypto input BTC/AUD" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>

<li>Click <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/general/transfer-trading.png')} alt="translte" width="30" />, enter the amount of <strong>fiat asset</strong> you want to get from this trade.</li>

<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/tradinig/instant-buy-sell/instant-sell-crypto/2.png')} alt="Enter fiat from sell" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>

<li>Select <strong>Sell</strong> then you can see the total fees, including price, fee charged by CoinByte and the amount of crypto acquisition.</li>
<li>Confirm and finish the selling.</li>

<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/tradinig/instant-buy-sell/instant-sell-crypto/3.png')} alt="Order filled sell" style={{ width: 300 }} />
